Currency Pipes

No Formatting : {{number }}
{{"USD Doller($): "+(number | currency: 'USD')}}
USD Doller : {{number | currency :'USD':'USD'}}
INR() : {{number | currency :'INR'}}
INR : {{number | currency :'INR':'INR'}}
